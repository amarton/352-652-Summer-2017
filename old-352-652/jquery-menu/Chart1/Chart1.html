Germany has played in every World Cup except in 1930 when they did not enter and 1950 when they were banned after the WWII. They've consistently performed at a high level in the FIFA World cup, earning second-place and third-place finishes four times each and one fourth-place finish. They've placed at least 3rd in the last four World Cups and has come very close to matching Brazil's performance of winning a total of five World Cups.
