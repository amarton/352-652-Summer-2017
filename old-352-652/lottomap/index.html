Marylanders buy lottery tickets in every county in the state, but the sales are not distributed equally. In Baltimore City, Prince George's County and Charles County, adults spent, on average, more than $500 per year on lottery tickets in 2012. In Maryland's two westernmost counties, adults spent less than $200 per year on average. Click on a county for more details.
